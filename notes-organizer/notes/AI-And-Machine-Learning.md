# Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning 

## A.I.

Artificial Intelligence, means a simulation of human intelligence. An A.I. is a machine that acts like a human.

**Narrow A.I.**: A machine can be just as good or even better than humans at specific tasks. Machines that can only do one thing really well.

**General A.I.**: Machines that can perform multiple abilities (also called AGI - Artificial General Intelligence), that doesn't exist yet.

## M.L.

Machine Learning is a subset of A.I., and machine learning is an approach to try and achieve artificial intelligence through systems that can find patterns in a set of data.

**Common ML Applications:**
- **Prediction**: Forecasting results based on incoming data
- **Classification**: Categorizing data into predefined groups
- **Clustering**: Grouping similar items together
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identifying unusual patterns or outliers
- **Recommendation**: Suggesting items based on preferences or behavior

**Data Science**

Analyzing data, looking at data, and then doing something with it, that usually achieves a business goal.
